
Currently planned .banner usage:
--------------------------------

. ...... only default image

.banner image

. ...... only default text

.banner text

. ...... default text and image side by side

.banner text image

. ...... specific text/image side by side

.banner text: mytext.html image: myimage.jpg

. ...... specific text/image with break (top/bottom)

.banner text: mytext.html // image: myimage.jpg


. ...... specific text/image, then break, then navbar

.banner text: mytext.html image: myimage.jpg // navbar

. ...... stacked text/image, then navbar

.banner text: (mytext.html // image: myimage.jpg) navbar


. ...... vert navbar, image, text

.banner vnavbar image text
